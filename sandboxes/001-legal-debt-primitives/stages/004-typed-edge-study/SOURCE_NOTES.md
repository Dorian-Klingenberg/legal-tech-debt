# Source Notes

This stage uses synthetic sample data. It is not legal authority and should not be used for legal interpretation.

The corpus borrows structural inspiration from official Kentucky insurance materials:

- Kentucky Revised Statutes Chapter 304, Insurance Code: https://apps.legislature.ky.gov/law/statutes/chapter.aspx?id=38708
- KRS 304.39-010, policy and purpose for the Motor Vehicle Reparations Act: https://apps.legislature.ky.gov/law/statutes/statute.aspx?id=30024
- 806 KAR 39:070, proof of motor vehicle insurance: https://apps.legislature.ky.gov/law/kar/titles/806/039/070/
- Kentucky Department of Insurance no-fault/PIP overview: https://insurance.ky.gov/ppc/newstatic_info.aspx?divid=15&static_id=24

The section bodies in `data/corpus` are invented for software testing. The numbering style, topics, and relationship shapes are meant to produce realistic dependency-analysis behavior, not authoritative legal text.

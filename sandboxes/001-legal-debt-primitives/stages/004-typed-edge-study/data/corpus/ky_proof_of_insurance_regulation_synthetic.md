# Synthetic Kentucky Proof of Insurance Regulation Excerpts

This file is synthetic sample data for dependency-analysis experiments. It is not legal authority.

## Section 806.39.070.1. Definitions

For this regulation, an insurer means a carrier authorized to issue a motor vehicle insurance policy under Section 304.39.080.
Proof of insurance has the meaning used in Section 304.39.117.

## Section 806.39.070.2. Proof of insurance to be provided by insurers

An insurer shall provide proof of insurance when a policy is issued, renewed, or amended to include a vehicle as required by Section 304.39.117.
Printed proof shall follow Section 806.39.070.3, and electronic proof shall follow Section 806.39.070.8.

## Section 806.39.070.3. Printed proof card format

Printed proof cards shall include the policy number, effective dates, vehicle information, and insurer name.
Card content shall support presentation to a county clerk or peace officer under Section 806.39.070.5 and the proof obligation in Section 304.39.117.

## Section 806.39.070.4. Electronic coverage reporting

Electronic coverage reporting shall use the policyholder and VIN data required by Section 304.39.087.
Termination feeds shall be reconciled with Section 304.39.085 and exception handling in Section 806.39.070.7.

## Section 806.39.070.5. Presentation to county clerk or peace officer

Presentation rules shall support proof of insurance under Section 304.39.117 and printed card content under Section 806.39.070.3.
If the insured relies on a digital card, the exception process in Section 1300 shall be followed.

## Section 806.39.070.6. Binder, contract, and commercial policy cancellation notices

Cancellation reporting for binders shall implement Section 304.39.083 and shall update termination reporting under Section 304.39.085.
The reporting file shall use the electronic coverage reporting method in Section 806.39.070.4.

## Section 806.39.070.7. Electronic reporting exceptions

If the electronic reporting feed fails, the insurer shall preserve records under Section 1500 and notify the proof-card owner under Section 1030.
The obsolete exception code table references Section 806.39.070.9.

## Section 806.39.070.8. Forms library and card templates

The forms library shall maintain printed card templates under Section 806.39.070.3 and electronic card templates under Section 806.39.070.2.
The library may reference ISO Personal Auto form edition 2024 when the form owner records the edition.
The library shall not rely on ISO circulars without a versioned source note.

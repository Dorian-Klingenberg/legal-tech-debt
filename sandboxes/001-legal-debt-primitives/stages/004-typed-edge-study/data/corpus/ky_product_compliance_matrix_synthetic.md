# Synthetic Kentucky Product Compliance Matrix

This file is synthetic sample data for dependency-analysis experiments. It is not legal authority.

## Section 2000. Product change intake

Product changes affecting Kentucky private passenger auto coverage shall screen Section 304.39.080, Section 304.39.100, Section 304.39.110, Section 304.20.020, and Section 806.39.070.8.

## Section 2010. Rate and rule filing screen

The filing screen shall decide whether a product change belongs in the forms library under Section 806.39.070.8 or in the adverse action notice workflow under Section 1100.
The screen shall also record any NAIC reference by bulletin year.

## Section 2020. Form ownership

Form ownership shall coordinate printed proof cards in Section 806.39.070.3, digital cards in Section 806.39.070.8, and template update review in Section 1080.

## Section 2030. Producer training

Producer training shall cover proof validation under Section 1030, producer communications under Section 1110, and printed fallback under Section 1400.

## Section 2040. Control map

The control map shall connect product intake in Section 2000, filing screen in Section 2010, form ownership in Section 2020, producer training in Section 2030, and supervisory review in Section 1600.

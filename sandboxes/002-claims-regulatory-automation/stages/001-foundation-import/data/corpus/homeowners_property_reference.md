# Synthetic Kentucky Homeowners Property Coverage Reference

## Section 100. Purpose

This reference establishes minimum structural review rules for Kentucky homeowners property policies.

## Section 200. Definitions

- "Covered dwelling" means a residence premises insured under a homeowners policy subject to this reference.
- "Legacy endorsement" means a homeowners endorsement form retired before January 1, 2020.
- "Policy year" means the annual period beginning on the policy effective date.

## Section 300. Minimum Property Coverage

An insurer shall describe dwelling coverage and covered property conditions as provided in Section 310.
Notices required by this section must comply with Section 900.

## Section 310. Coverage Amounts

Coverage amounts shall follow NAIC Model Law guidance unless a more specific Kentucky property rule applies.
The disclosure template must follow ISO homeowners form language.

## Section 400. Claim Notice

Claim notice timing is governed by Section 410.

## Section 410. Claim Notice Exception

Exceptions to claim notice timing are governed by Section 400.

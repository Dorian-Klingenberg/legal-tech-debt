# Synthetic Internal Policy Manual

## Section 10. Authority

This manual implements Section 300 and Section 310 of the state minimum coverage statute.

## Section 20. Required Action

When a claim is denied for insufficient coverage, the adjuster shall send notice within 10 business days.
The notice workflow references Section 30.

## Section 30. Evidence

The claim file must include policy version, denial reason, notice date, and reviewer initials.

## Section 40. External Guidance

The claims team should consult NAIC guidance and ISO circulars when updating templates.

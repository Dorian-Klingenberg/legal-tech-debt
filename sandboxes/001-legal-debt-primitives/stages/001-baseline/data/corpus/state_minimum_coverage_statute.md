# Synthetic State Minimum Coverage Statute

## Section 100. Purpose

This chapter establishes minimum coverage rules for private passenger auto policies.

## Section 200. Definitions

- "Covered vehicle" means a vehicle insured under a policy subject to this chapter.
- "Legacy endorsement" means an endorsement form retired before January 1, 2020.
- "Policy year" means the annual period beginning on the policy effective date.

## Section 300. Minimum Coverage

An insurer shall provide minimum liability coverage as described in Section 310.
Notices required by this section must comply with Section 900.

## Section 310. Coverage Amounts

Coverage amounts shall follow NAIC Model Law guidance unless a more specific state rule applies.
The disclosure template must follow ISO form language.

## Section 400. Claim Notice

Claim notice timing is governed by Section 410.

## Section 410. Claim Notice Exception

Exceptions to claim notice timing are governed by Section 400.
